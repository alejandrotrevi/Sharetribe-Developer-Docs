# Introduction
Sharetribe is a complete solution for building powerful online marketplaces. Sharetribe offers all the necessary marketplace infrastructure out of the box while allowing you full control to customize your user experience. As your business grows, Sharetribe takes care of running and scaling your backend. Powered by modern technology (React, Redux, and Node.js) and designed around a headless architecture, Sharetribe is fast, scalable, and customizable.
